About Us

DC Decorating & Contractors Ltd

Established in 2014, D&C Decorating Contractors provides professional painting services throughout the North West.

A little bit about us

D&C Decorating Contractors specialise in professional painting services for the private and commercial sectors. We have a dedicated team of qualified professionals working throughout the North West providing a range of painting services. Our services are extensive,ranging from commercial regeneration projects to bespoke domestic work. We specialise in commercial contracting services and are currently providing contract solutions to a number of large businesses in the North West.
